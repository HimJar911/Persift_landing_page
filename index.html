Persift — Auto-Apply to Jobs with AI

Persift is a Chrome extension that automatically finds early-career jobs and internships before they hit LinkedIn, tailors your resume for each role, and submits applications on your behalf — on Greenhouse, Ashby, and Lever ATSes.

What Persift does

  • Finds early-career roles and internships the moment they're posted — before they appear on LinkedIn or Indeed
  • Analyzes each job against your profile and resume for a match score
  • Tailors your resume and cover letter for each specific role automatically
  • Auto-fills and submits job applications on Greenhouse, Ashby, and Lever
  • Runs overnight so you wake up to submitted applications and interview requests

Who it's for

Persift is built for early-career job seekers — college students, new graduates, and anyone applying to their first or second job. If you're applying to internships or entry-level roles and tired of filling out the same form 200 times, Persift automates the process end to end.

How it works

  1. Install the Persift Chrome extension from the Chrome Web Store
  2. Set up your profile once — name, university, role types, locations, and resume
  3. Persift finds matching roles, tailors your application, and auto-applies
  4. Check your dashboard each morning for submitted applications, responses, and interview invites

Supported job platforms

Persift auto-applies on Greenhouse, Ashby, and Lever — the three most common ATS platforms used by tech companies for internship and new grad hiring.

Frequently asked questions

Does Persift automatically apply to jobs?

Yes. Persift finds matching jobs and submits complete applications automatically, including resume upload, form fields, and short-answer questions.

Is Persift a Chrome extension?

Yes. Persift is a Chrome browser extension. Install it once from the Chrome Web Store and it runs in the background.

What kind of jobs does Persift apply to?

Persift focuses on early-career roles — software engineering internships, product internships, design internships, data internships, and new grad positions at tech companies.

How is Persift different from other job application tools?

Most auto-apply tools blast the same generic application everywhere. Persift tailors each application to the specific role — matching your experience to the job requirements and writing custom responses — so applications look human and specific, not spammy.
